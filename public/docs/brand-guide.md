# 🔍 Brand Guide: Check Your Photo (MetaRead)
**Version:** 1.0  
**Core Philosophy:** "We show you what's in the file. You decide what it means."

---

## 1. Brand Identity & Voice
### Personality
*   **Objective:** We are a neutral mirror. We don't judge; we report.
*   **Sharp:** High technical precision, forensic-grade terminology.
*   **Transparent:** We prioritize the "Zero-Storage" architecture as a core trust feature.
*   **Cybernetic:** A bridge between raw digital headers and human understanding.

### Tone of Voice
*   **Use:** *Forensic Audit, Metadata Headers, Cryptographic Fingerprint, Transitory Processing, Anomaly Matrix.*
*   **Avoid:** *Verification, Truth, Real, Fake, Authentic.* (These are user inferences, not our claims).

---

## 2. Visual Identity
### Color Palette (Forensic High-Contrast)
*   **Deep Obsidian (`#101314`):** Primary background.
*   **Forensic Green (`#00FF8C`):** Primary accent.
*   **Alert Red (`#FF4D4D`):** High-risk anomalies.
*   **Warning Orange (`#FFA500`):** Medium-risk discrepancies.
*   **Data Gray (`#E0E0E0`):** Primary text.

### Typography
*   **The Brand Hero:** `Big Shoulders Display` (900 Weight). 
*   **The System Interface:** `Silkscreen`. 
*   **The Technical HUD:** `Orbitron`.
*   **The Reading Standard:** `Trebuchet MS`.

---

## 3. UI/UX Principles
### The "Matrix" Aesthetic
*   **Background Grids:** 50px subtle green grid.
*   **Scan Lines:** Animated horizontal laser lines.
*   **Focus Markers:** Crosshair svgs.

---

## 4. Brand Assets
*   **Logo:** Telescope Icon (Scanning) and Microscope Icon (Analysis).
*   **Tagline:** `PRIVACY_ENFORCED // ZERO_STORAGE_POLICY`

---

## 5. Compliance & Trust
*   **Zero-Storage Disclaimer:** Must be visible before any data dispatch.
*   **Hypothesis Warning:** Always remind users that metadata can be forged.
