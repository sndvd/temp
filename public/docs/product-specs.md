# 🔍 Product Description: Check Your Photo (MetaRead)

### **Executive Summary**
Check Your Photo (MetaRead) is a professional-grade forensic metadata reporting tool designed to provide absolute transparency into digital media files.

### **Core Promise**
*"We extract and display every hidden metadata header from your photos and documents. You decide what it means."*

### **Key Value Propositions**
1.  **Neutral Reporting:** Raw data and forensic indicators without subjective verdicts.
2.  **Privacy-First Architecture:** Zero-Storage Protocol (processed in RAM, destroyed instantly).
3.  **AI-Assisted Translation:** Complex headers translated into plain-English forensic summaries.
4.  **Anomaly Matrix:** Flags inconsistencies like clock tampering and AI generation.

---

# 🛠️ Technical Specifications

### **1. Software Stack**
*   **Runtime:** Node.js 20+
*   **Metadata Engine:** `exiftool`
*   **AI Engine:** OpenAI GPT-4o
*   **Frontend:** Bootstrap 5 (Forensic Dark Theme)
*   **Mapping:** Leaflet.js
*   **Reports:** Puppeteer (PDF) / Resend (Email)

### **2. Extraction Capabilities**
*   **Supported Formats:** JPEG, PNG, HEIC, HEIF, WEBP, TIFF, PDF.
*   **Hashing:** MD5 and SHA-256 integrity fingerprints.

### **3. Forensic Detection Logic**
*   **AI Trace Detection:** Keywords and `trainedAlgorithmicMedia` tags.
*   **Tamper Patterns:** Chronological impossibilities.
*   **Software Markers:** Photoshop, GIMP, Canva detection.
*   **Heuristics:** Screenshot detection based on resolution ratios.

### **4. Security & Compliance**
*   **Memory Storage:** Files never written to disk.
*   **No Persistence:** No media stored in databases.
*   **No Training:** Data never used for AI training.
